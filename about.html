About

I was born April 9th, 1994 in Freehold, New Jersey
My home town is the small shore town of Keyport, New Jersey
I'm currently studying Interactive Multimedia at the College of New Jersey, with minors in Graphic Design and Marketing
My artistic interests lie in graphic design, web and application development, and surreal, conceptual photography

Artist's Statement

Before I ever sat down in a class room, I was learning. I always figured the best way to learn something was to experience it, and I believe that’s how my creativity developed. From the first photoshop file I opened, the first line of code I wrote, or the the first camera I held in my hands, I was on my own, driven by pure curiosity and the desire to learn something new. And as I developed these skills, unbridled by the restrictions of a classroom , I realized how much I enjoyed accomplishing the goals I set for myself and improving my skills. It wasn’t until I got older that I realized this creativity I enjoyed so much could one day become a part of my career and began to further my education in the classroom.

No matter whether I’m designing or developing, photographing or writing, I always strive to take the most creative and unique approach to my work that I can imagine. I don’t stop until I’m completely satisfied, and then I keep working to make it better. I’m inspired by the constantly changing society around us to update and improve what I create, motivated that I could be a part of that change. A good artist should always be improving, never stopping until they are truly proud of their work. As long as I am proud of my work, I know I have done well.
